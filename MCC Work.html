Understanding MCC, Intersect, and the human side of decentralized governance

What is MCC?

Governance Community Intersect

The Membership & Community Committee (MCC) plays a key role in strengthening participation within the Cardano ecosystem. It focuses on onboarding, engagement, and ensuring members are not just registered — but active contributors.

“To belong is to be seen. Governance is not just code, it is people.”

The Human Layer of Blockchain

While blockchain systems emphasize decentralization and automation, real governance depends on people. Communities grow when individuals are empowered to participate meaningfully, not just observe.

MCC helps bridge this gap by connecting structure (Intersect governance) with real-world participation.

Why It Matters

  • Transforms passive members into active contributors
  • Improves accessibility to governance systems
  • Strengthens decentralization through participation
  • Builds long-term ecosystem resilience
